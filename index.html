One-Month Course Syllabus


Week 1 :- Foundation of Cinematography
Week 2 :- Advanced Techniques and Photography Basics
Week 3 :- Color Theory, Gimbal and Camera Moves

Eleven-Months Course Syllabus


Month 1 - 6 :- Foundation in Cinematography
  • History of Cinema and Film Appreciation

    Explore the evolution of cinema and its profound impact on society and culture. Analyze iconic films, decode cinematic language, and gain a deeper appreciation for the art of storytelling.

  • Photography Fundamentals

    Master the fundamentals of photography, laying the groundwork for visual storytelling and cinematography.

  • Advanced Cinematography Techniques

    From lighting and composition to motion control and editing, delve into advanced techniques that push the boundaries of visual storytelling.

Month 7 - 12 :- Advanced Cinematography and Web series Productions
  • Cinematography Post Production

    Explore the nuances of post-production, harnessing the power of cutting-edge technology to elevate your storytelling.

  • Pre-Production for Web Series Production

    Bring your creative vision to life as you delve into the pre-production process for web series production. Develop concepts, scout locations, and craft budgets under the guidance of industry professionals.

  • Industry Exposure and Overseas Trip

    Immerse yourself in the global film industry with an unforgettable overseas trip. Gain firsthand experience in international filmmaking practices and forge connections that last a lifetime.

  • Feature Film or Web Series Production

    Collaborate with your peers to produce a feature film or web series, applying your newfound skills to create captivating content that resonates with audiences worldwide.

Finally:- Assessment and Evaluation
  • Real-world Projects

    Showcase your skills through hands-on projects, including the production of a feature film or web series.

  • Industry Visits

    Gain insights from industry experts through visits to production studios, post-production houses, and film sets.

  • Global Perspective

    Broaden your horizons with an overseas trip that exposes you to diverse cultures and filmmaking practices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Provide more details about the overseas trip offered by Cinema Factory?

The overseas trip is a unique opportunity for students to gain international exposure and experience. It typically spans three days and includes immersive activities related to the film industry, such as workshops, film screenings, or visits to renowned film studios. This trip allows students to broaden their horizons, network with professionals, and gain insights into global filmmaking practices.

What courses does Cinema Factory offer?

Cinema Factory offers a one-year course in Screenplay and Direction, Digital Cinematography, Film Editing and VFX, Acting, and Choreography as weekend courses. Cinema Factory will offer special one-month professional filmmaking courses soon at various parts of South India.

How is the course structured?

The first six months cover the major portions of the syllabus, while the second term focuses on industry visits and filming a web series.

Are there any financial assistance options available for students?

Yes, Cinema Factory offers EMI facilities to students.

What certifications do students receive upon completion of the course?

Students receive an NFDC certificate upon completion of the course.

Are there placement opportunities available for graduates?

Cinema Factory strives to offer placements in the film and media industry on a requirement basis.

What opportunities do students have for practical experience?

Students have the opportunity to shoot a web series while studying and participate in a 3-day overseas trip.

Who teaches the courses at Cinema Factory?

Our regular faculties include award-winning filmmakers who bring their expertise to the classroom.
